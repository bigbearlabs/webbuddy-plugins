Hi, I'm WebBuddy.

I can help you keep focus.

I work with hyperlinks (a.k.a. URLs, or just 'links') in apps you depend on to get work done, like Mail, Twitter and Evernote.

I can show you the page within your workflow, rather than throwing you into a browser window where you probably have a bunch of other things open, so you can easily go back to what you were doing.

I'm pretty low-key.

I live in your menu bar. Sometimes I might be hidden if your menu bar's crowded, but that should be okay - you need to find me there only if you need to set preferences, or to peek at your last opened page.

I play well with others.

If you need to start a mini-workflow from a page you opened with me, I can dispatch that link to a browser of your choice.

If you want to open a link in a particular browser, click the link while holding down the [alt/option] or [alt/option] + [shift] modifier key(s) to send the link to a browser of your choice, configured in the preferences.

Some other details.

I'll be your default browser while I'm running.

When I quit, I'll restore your previous default browser setting. So if you like what I do, please keep me running!

I hope you find me useful - I also hear the people who made me would really like to hear how it works out!
